Who created City Guesser?
City Guesser was created and launched by Paul McBurney Jr. He was inspired by one of his favorite games, GeoGuessr, and wanted to bring value to the geography community. He first shared the game to Reddit on August 14th, 2020 and it turned out to be a hit. Since then, we've been growing and maintaining an amazing community.

Why are there no ads?
Providing the best user experience is one of the core values behind City Guesser. You can donate to help cover server costs, especially for multiplayer.
How can I contribute?
We appreciate anyone who takes the time to submit video or donate. For most people, the most effective thing you can do is share City Guesser with a friend. Maybe even play a round of multiplayer with them!
How do I submit my own video?
If you'd like to have your video on City Guesser, post it to YouTube with the title "City Guesser Video:" and the city being showcased. Example: City Guesser Video: Barcelona, Spain
How can I help financially?
City Guesser has never ran a single ad. This is why. You can donate here to keep City Guesser free.
