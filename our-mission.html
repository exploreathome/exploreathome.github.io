About Us

why was virtualvacation.us created?

We created the first iteration of VirtualVacation within 4 weeks to help people experience the wonders of the world while in lockdown. VirtualVacation started whilst the entire world was quarantined and unable to travel, therefore, we wanted to create something that would give everyone the ability to explore different cities. As time went on, we added new cities, modes of travel, and even developed a game that lets you travel, and challenge your mind at the same time.

Furthermore, we wanted to create a hub where people could access unedited/uncut footage from around the world. Not only this, but, virtualvacation.us serves as a central hub for finding videos catered to each and every city. This means you won't have to go scowering through different video sharing platforms to find a city what you want to sightsee!

how can I help contribute to the site?

The main way you can contribute is to provide us unedited footage from cities around the world. If you own the footage and wish to submit it to us, please contact us via our email! Don't hesitate, just send something in and it's likely that we will accept it. Happy sightseeing!

Also, sharing really helps too!
