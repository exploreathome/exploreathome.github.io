Helpful Information

1. How do I get good at this game?

On behalf of City Guesser, we have some tips, and things to look out for when playing. Look out for:
- Vehicle Plates
- Restaurant Names
- Road Markings/Side
- Vegetation/Climate
- Flags (Beware of Consulates)
- Speech (Identify Languages)

2. Why did my score disappear?

Streaks Mode is currently in beta, as a result, an official login system is yet to be incorporated. Scores are stored using localstorage. If you clear your cookies, your scores will be deleted as you just told the browser to remove LocalStorage details, and all existing cookies.
