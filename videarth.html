About VidEarth

What is VidEarth?

VidEarth, brought to you by virtualvacation.us, welcomes users to explore the Earth from home. Virtual Vacation was created amid the need to travel during the COVID-19 Pandemic. We recently launched VidEarth on May 27, 2021. Through this platform, we hope to introduce you to new cultures, architecture, and a greater understanding of the world. You can explore more immersive webapps like this one, at our homepage.
